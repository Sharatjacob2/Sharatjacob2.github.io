THE SQUARE'S TAKE

TIS NOT FOR THE FAINT OF HEART

The Square

I go by many names, unvoluntarily. Some call me Sharat, some call me Jacob Square, I myself go by Square. My parents call me Sharathoo. I aspire to create worlds through the fields of literature where I dictate the rules.
For more things about me that you did not need to know, click on my nose.

Short Stories

There's a story for every mood. Not here, but somewhere out there on the internet. I am not going to help you find them. Instead, come over and read my stories, because you can always spare a minute to let your imagination run wild.
Stories, because they are worth it. (Most of the time)

Diary

This was created virtue of a terrible idea that originated one night during quarantine. I asked myself, "What could possibly be more worse that being miserable in quarantine, stuck with nowhere to go?"
Telling everyone that I am miserable in quarantine, stuck with nowhere to go, daily.

Incoming

The future lies ahead. I lie here, contemplating future possible projects. Coming up with abstract ideas for stories, contemplating about writing screenplays, novels, mini-novels etc. Take a look at the previews of what might be reality someday.

Suggestions
