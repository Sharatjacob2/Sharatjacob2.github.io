Welcome to the Square's Take.
This is a collection of all my written works, which include diary entries, stories etc.

I created this as a method to keep and view everything I write in one easily accessible place for anyone who would "like" to read them. All the links take your device all the way to Medium, a destination that you may not have intended to reach when you embarked on the journey of "let me check out Sharat's blog for a second."
You are now trapped. Enjoy your time here.
The diary entries can be found in the DIARY section, separated in two installments, THE DAYS and THE NIGHTS.
The stories can be found in, naturally (posh slang for duh!), the STORIES section.
Incoming stories can be found in the incoming section. If you like any of them, tell me (although I don't believe I have designed a way for you to do this).

Now, to make up more stuff to fill up all this empty stuff, some info about yours truly.
I am currently 19 years old at the onset of this blog. It's quarantine in 2020. With all this free time, I have begun to extend my creative tendrils to do whatever I wanna do, like many in this period of time. I hope to be one of many that have attempted to dig deep and unleash imaginative works of art. Maybe once this quarantine is over, we may have jumpstarted a new Renaissance.
